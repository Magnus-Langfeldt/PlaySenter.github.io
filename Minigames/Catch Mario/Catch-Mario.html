Catch Mario

Mario is on the loose! Known for his lightning-fast reflexes and cunning tactics, Mario has been evading capture, leaving a trail of stolen treasures in his wake.

It's your duty to apprehend the elusive thief Mario and bring him to justice! Equipped with only your mouse, fingers and keen observation skills, you must capture him.

Mario is currently hiding in the wall, but beware! He has planted bombs in some of the holes, so be careful!

Keep your eyes peeled and your reflexes sharp, for every successful click on Mario brings you closer to restoring peace and order to Mushroomopolis.

Will you be able to outsmart the master thief Mario and claim victory for law and order?

Use your mouse or fingers to catch him! Click on Mario to get points, but if you hit a bomb, you lose!
